Stream of Time

Every story, Every Moment, In Real Time

Stream of Time is a news aggregation platform that scans Zambian media outlets for civil court news and related stories. It automatically collects, classifies, and organizes articles from multiple sources into a single, easy-to-browse feed.

How It Works

The system periodically scans news websites and uses keyword-based classification to identify relevant articles. Articles are categorized into court, politics, business, crime, social, and general topics. Court articles are further classified by case type — land, contract, labour, family, debt, defamation, and more.
